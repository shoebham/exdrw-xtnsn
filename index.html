Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30th June 2024

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how our Excalidraw in New Tab Extension ("Extension") collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information Collection and Use

Our Extension does not collect any personal information. It only stores the Excalidraw drawing data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data is used solely to persist your drawings between browser sessions.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, nor do we transmit it to any external servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
